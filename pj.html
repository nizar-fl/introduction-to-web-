ABOUT US


Jewellery, to most women, is extremely precious. To some, necklaces might seem like yet another piece of jewellery; but to those who love dressing up, it's more than just a simple accessory. If you too, have a liking for statement necklaces, you don't have to look any further. NECKNIZ offers a splendid collection of ornaments like necklace sets in a wide assortment of colours and patterns. Ranging from the subtle to the extravagant, there is something for everyone.

Pretty simple gold plated necklace set:
These are simple chains that are made up of gold plating. These are the necklace types that the women prefer to wear daily. Due to the simplicity of the design pattern it is always easy to carry them on every day basis. These pretty chains can be worn by pair up with little cute pendants or lockets that come in the centre of the neck.
Princess necklace set:
Most commonly worn by women and the brides too.!! They usually come with a length varying between seventeen to nineteen inches. They look good with almost all types of outfits like sarees, half sarees, lehengas, ethnic kurtis. These usually come up with earrings that match the design of the neckpiece.
Multi coloured string necklace set:
These multi-stringed necklaces add in a classy look to your attire. These are current hot favourites of college going girls and also party goers. They look perfect on light coloured clothes as they usually come in bright combination of colours. Wearing them with light coloured kurtis, adds in the perfect twist to the whole look.

Plastron necklace set:
These are heavy mirror type jewellery, that are perfect when worn on sleek long kurtis, that have Pakistani cut towards the ends. They can even be worn on those tops for the disco lighted evenings. They are long when compared to that of the regular necklaces and cover the neck above the bust.
Bib necklace set:
Made with layers of blings and beads. These really bold chunky necklace can be paired up with simple tops. These add the classy funky look to your outfit. These necklace are replacements to the old style scarf that came with some small neckpieces. Looks best when wore with deep neck tops, kurtis or neckless dresses.
Matinee necklace set:
The matinee necklace looks at its best for the wide necked outfits. They cone with length of twenty to twenty two inches. This necklace hangs just above the neck part.
Thread necklace set:
These current trendy artificial necklaces are made up of different colourful threads that are knotted and that consists of oxidised pendants and hangings. These fusion set of necklace can be worn with sarees, kurtis. They go in well with almost every kind of outfit. Mostly preferred by women wearing sarees, as it gives a whole dignified and classy look.
Choker:
These are worn very close to the neckline. They go in well with modern styled tops, kurtis and sarees too. They come in the length varying from fourteen to sixteen inches. They can glam up the look, be it for any outfit and any occasion.
